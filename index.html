Resilient Underground

Stay Grounded. Stay Strong.

Built for People Who’ve Been Through Something

Resilient Underground is for people who’ve faced adversity, rebuilt themselves, and are still pushing forward. This isn’t surface-level motivation — this is real conversation about discipline, mental health, sobriety, fatherhood, service, and life after hard seasons.

About the Host

I’m Steven Littlefield — a Marine Corps veteran, father, and someone who has navigated anxiety, substance struggles, and the process of rebuilding a life with purpose.

This podcast was created to give a voice to the conversations most people avoid — the internal battles, the discipline it takes to stay grounded, and what it actually looks like to move forward.

Topics We Cover

  • Resilience and discipline
  • Mental health and anxiety
  • Sobriety and rebuilding
  • Fatherhood and responsibility
  • Life after military service
  • Purpose and personal growth
